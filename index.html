About Ruben Duijn

Ruben Duijn is the Founder & Managing Director of BlueWater Strategy, a Switzerland-based SAP, analytics, and AI advisory firm. He brings more than 23 years of experience in reporting, planning, and transformation work, with delivery depth across BW, BW/4HANA, S/4HANA, SAC, BPC Embedded, IBP/IP, ABAP OO, CDS Views, HANA, Fiori, and finance-oriented analytics architecture.

The company’s philosophy is to connect strong information foundations with better management decisions: first through Business Warehouse and reporting, then through planning and performance management, and finally through Applied Intelligence that improves workflows, visibility, and customer communication. This approach has been shaped by work across banking, insurance, manufacturing, transport, retail, and other data-intensive environments.

BlueWater Strategy combines architecture thinking, delivery discipline, and business pragmatism - helping organisations modernize SAP reporting, strengthen planning capability, and adopt AI where it creates clearer insight, stronger online presence, and measurable commercial value.
