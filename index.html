About Ruben Duijn

Ruben Duijn is the Founder & Managing Director of BlueWater Strategy, a Switzerland-based SAP, analytics, and AI advisory firm. He brings more than 23 years of experience in analytics, planning, reporting, and transformation work, with delivery depth across BW, BW/4HANA, S/4HANA reporting, SAC, BPC Embedded, ABAP OO, CDS views, and finance-oriented data architecture.

The company’s delivery approach draws on experience built through SAP AG, BearingPoint, EY, Sopra Steria, and independent advisory work for clients in banking, insurance, manufacturing, transport, retail, and other data-intensive sectors. This creates a strong mix of architecture thinking, delivery discipline, and business pragmatism.

Current work also includes applied AI for LLM-assisted knowledge workflows, retrieval-based assistants, reporting copilots, automation, and customer-facing digital presence - including websites, content structuring, online positioning, and communication support. The emphasis remains business-first: use AI where it improves clarity, speed, visibility, and commercial usefulness.
