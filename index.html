About Ruben Duijn

Ruben Duijn is the Founder & Managing Director of BlueWater Strategy, a Switzerland-based SAP, analytics, and AI advisory firm. He brings more than 23 years of experience solving reporting, planning, and transformation challenges across BW, BW/4HANA, S/4HANA, SAC, BPC Embedded, IBP/IP, ABAP OO, CDS Views, HANA, Fiori, and finance-oriented analytics architecture.

His track record includes SAP AG, BearingPoint, EY, Sopra Steria, and selected mandates across banking, insurance, manufacturing, transport, retail, and other data-intensive environments. Clients engage him when a landscape needs sharper architecture judgment, calmer execution, and real hands-on delivery rather than generic advice.
