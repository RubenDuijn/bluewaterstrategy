# BlueWater Strategy - General Rules

## Company Focus Areas
BlueWater Strategy focuses on three core areas:
1. **SAP Transformation** — SAP landscape modernization through SAC, BW/4HANA, S/4HANA, and related services.
2. **AI Intelligent Automation** — Custom AI models, machine learning, predictive analytics, copilots, and RPA orchestration.
3. **Real Estate Development** — Property acquisition, renovation planning, positioning, leasing, and asset value creation.

The three pillars serve as the internal structure for website design and content organization. In customer-facing messaging, emphasize integrated, end-to-end delivery and full-package outcomes. Do not lead with how many areas we cover — lead with what we deliver. Keep the structure clear, but avoid silo thinking in solutions and proposals.

Note: SAP Transformation and AI Intelligent Automation have a natural degree of overlap — for example, AI embedded in SAP analytics or intelligent automation within SAP workflows. Real Estate Development is the most standalone pillar and should generally be positioned separately.

## Content Rules
- Use clear, business-focused language.
- Structure internal content around the three pillars; present to customers as a seamless, integrated offering.
- When SAP is mentioned as a separate topic, explicitly include AI automation potential in the same context when relevant.
- In combined offering phrasing, prefer "SAP, AI, or Real Estate" (not "and Real Estate") to reflect that Real Estate is typically positioned separately.
- Prefer consistent capitalization: Real Estate, SAP HANA, BW/4HANA, S/4HANA, SAC.
- Avoid exaggerated claims unless verified with data.
- Keep paragraphs short for readability on desktop and mobile.

## Design Rules
- Keep section spacing and heading styles consistent.
- Use the same card structure and visual rhythm across sections.
- Keep image sizes aligned within card groups.
- Preserve a clean, professional Swiss-business visual style.
- Ensure strong color contrast for text readability.

## Navigation Rules
- Every navigation link must map to an existing section ID.
- Use smooth scrolling and keep anchor alignment below the sticky header.
- Maintain scroll-margin-top on linked sections.

## Image Rules
- Store all website images inside the images/ folder.
- Use relative paths (example: images/filename.jpg).
- Confirm each referenced image exists.
- Keep alt text meaningful and concise.

## Copy Consistency Rules
- Use one version of repeated terms across the site.
- Keep terminology uniform across hero, cards, and about sections.
- When updating a sentence in one section, check for stale variants elsewhere.

## Quality Check Before Publish
- Verify all links and section anchors.
- Verify mobile layout (hero, cards, and spacing).
- Check for broken image paths.
- Check spelling and capitalization consistency.
- Confirm local preview works before publishing.

## Hosting and Domain
- The domain http://bluewaterstrategy.ch is registered with Hostpoint.
- The website project is linked to GitHub at https://github.com/RubenDuijn/bluewaterstrategy.
- Keep deployment guidance aligned with the current publishing approach and domain configuration.
- Do not assume a hosting provider change unless explicitly confirmed.

## Change Management Rules
- Make small, focused edits and avoid scope creep.
- Preserve existing structure unless a redesign is explicitly requested.
- Re-check all affected sections after each change.
- Maintain version control backups before major changes.

## Delivery Approach
- Agile project delivery is the standard methodology and can be referenced in messaging where appropriate.
- Agile principles apply across all three pillars: SAP Transformation, AI Intelligent Automation, and Real Estate Development.
- Where the nature of a project calls for a more structured or phased approach, this can be noted, but agile remains the default framing.
